About Us

Hi! We are a small cageless CFA registered cattery in Manila specializing in tabbies and bicolor Exotic Shorthair cats.

Our cats are family members and they live freely in our home. They are pedigreed and registered with the CFA system.
You can see them on www.instagram.com/cleotheexplorer, where we post daily pictures.
If you'd like to inquire, please send a private message, or an email with your name and tell us a bit about yourself and your home.
